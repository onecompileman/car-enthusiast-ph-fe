Terms and Community Guidelines

Please review these rules before creating your account.

Platform Terms

  • You must provide accurate profile details and keep your account secure.
  • You are responsible for all activity posted through your account.
  • Only upload photos, videos, and content that you own or are allowed to share.
  • Do not post illegal modifications, falsified specs, or misleading build claims.
  • We may suspend accounts that violate platform rules or abuse other members.

Community Guidelines

  • Respect all enthusiasts regardless of build style, budget, or experience level.
  • No hate speech, harassment, threats, or explicit content.
  • Keep comments constructive, technical, and relevant to the build discussion.
  • Use accurate car details to help members discover and learn from your setup.
  • Report abusive or unsafe behavior so moderators can review it quickly.

By continuing registration, you agree to follow these rules to keep Car Enthusiast PH safe and useful for everyone.
